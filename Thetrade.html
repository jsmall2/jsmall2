The Trade

On June 29, 2016, Subban was traded to the Nashville Predators in exchange for defenceman Shea Weber. The signs were all there, as they have been for years, but it still seemed unthinkable that the Montreal Canadiens would choose to end their relationship with P.K. Subban so early in his NHL career. Until they did. Canadiens general manager Marc Bergevin saw an opportunity to add defenseman Shea Weber and jumped at it, trading Subban to the Nashville Predators on Wednesday, two days before a no-movement clause in his contract kicked in that covered the final six years of his agreement.
